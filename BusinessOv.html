Terms & Conditions

Renters must meet our eligibility requirements and agree to the terms outlined below before booking a vehicle. By renting from us, you accept responsibility for the car during the rental period and agree to return it in the same condition it was received. You’re also responsible for any fines, penalties, or damages incurred while the car is in your possession.

If you need to cancel your booking, please refer to our cancellation policy. Cancellations made at least 48 hours in advance will receive a full refund. Cancellations made with less notice may be subject to a partial refund or no refund depending on the timing.

Full payment is required at the time of booking. We accept major credit cards, G-cash, and cash with valid identification and security deposit. Rental rates include basic insurance, but additional coverage is available for purchase.

Privacy Policy

To rent a vehicle, we collect certain personal information including your name, contact details, driver license, and payment information. This data is securely stored and used only for rental purposes, identity verification, and processing payments. We do not sell your information. We may share it with third parties only when required by law or for payment processing and insurance purposes.

Rental Requirements

To rent a vehicle, you must be at least 21 years old and hold a valid driver’s license. We accept both local and international licenses. A security deposit is required at the start of the rental. This can be paid by credit card or in cash. The amount varies depending on the type of vehicle.

Rental Rules & Guidelines

Please return the vehicle with a full tank of gas, unless otherwise agreed upon. Driving is restricted to designated service areas; taking the vehicle outside these areas may result in additional charges or cancellation of the rental agreement. Speeding, reckless driving, or any traffic violations are strictly prohibited and will incur penalties. Smoking and transporting pets in the vehicle are not allowed unless explicitly permitted. Off-road use or towing with the rental vehicle is also not allowed.

Damage and Insurance Policy

Our rentals include basic insurance coverage for accidents, theft, and minor damage. However, this does not cover incidents caused by negligence, illegal activity, or violations of our rental terms. Additional insurance options are available for those who want extra protection. In the event of damage, a deductible will apply, and the renter may be held liable up to the full cost depending on the situation.

Price Lists & Cancellations

For late returns, we allow a 1-hour grace period. After that, additional charges apply either by the hour or a full day’s rate, depending on how long the return is delayed. Repeated or excessive delays may result in further penalties.

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled pick-up time qualify for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a partial fee. Rebooking is possible but may include extra charges depending on vehicle availability and timing.
