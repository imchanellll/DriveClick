FAQ's

WHAT KEY INFORMATION DO YOU COLLECT FROM A COSTUMER WHEN MAKING A RESERVATION? ▲
When a customer makes a reservation, we collect basic details like full name, contact number, driver’s license info, rental dates, and the delivery or pickup location.
WHAT IS INSURANCE COVERAHE DO YOU OFFER OR REQUIRE COSTUMER TO HAVE? ▲
We include basic insurance with every rental. Customers can also opt for comprehensive coverage at an extra charge. Alternatively, if they have personal coverage, we can consider that too.
DO COSTUMERS NEED TO SIGN ANY LEGAL DOCUMENTS OR WAIVERS BEFORE RENTING? ▲
Yes, we have a simple rental agreement/waiver that customers sign before the car is released. It outlines responsibilities, liabilities, and insurance coverage.
ARE THERE ANY RESTRICTION ON WHO CAN RENT? ▲
Renters must be at least 21 years old, have a valid driver’s license (at least 1 year old), and a clean driving record. For young or new drivers, we may request a co-renter or add extra conditions.
